Addis Safe House

Addis Safe House (ASH) is an international charity situated in the capital of Ethiopia, Addis Ababa. The charity helps tackle prostitution in the capital, by providing a safe house for the women, so they can get off the streets. Also the charity provides education and support so the women can stay off the streets.
